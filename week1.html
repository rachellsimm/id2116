Dustbin

/ˈdʌst.bɪn/

A container for household refuse, especially
one kept outside; a garbage can.


Dustbins exist everywhere in our home, school,
workplace and public spaces. Too often, they are
being despised and ignored in the modern society
because they are dirty and their unpleasant smell.

*Objective:
To retink trash-throwing experience and give people a sense of unexpectedness and delightfulness as people interact with the bin.
